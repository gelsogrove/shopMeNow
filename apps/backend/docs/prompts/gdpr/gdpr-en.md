# Privacy Notice and GDPR - English

## 1. Data Controller

The management of your personal data is carried out by the data controller indicated during registration to the ShopME service.

## 2. Purpose of Processing

Your personal data is processed for the following purposes:
- Provide communication services via WhatsApp
- Manage orders and commercial transactions
- Send notifications and service updates
- Comply with legal and tax obligations
- Improve user experience

## 3. Legal Basis

The processing of your data is based on:
- Contract performance (Art. 6, par. 1, lit. b GDPR)
- Legitimate interest (Art. 6, par. 1, lit. f GDPR)
- Legal compliance (Art. 6, par. 1, lit. c GDPR)

## 4. Categories of Data Processed

- Identification data (name, surname, phone number, email)
- Communication data (WhatsApp messages, chat history)
- Commercial data (orders, product preferences)
- Location data (country of origin from phone number)
- Technical data (IP, device type, cookies)

## 5. Data Recipients

Your data may be shared with:
- WhatsApp service providers (Meta Platforms)
- Hosting and cloud infrastructure providers
- Commercial partners and service providers
- Public authorities when required by law

## 6. Data Retention Period

Data is retained for:
- The entire duration of the commercial relationship
- 10 years for tax and accounting obligations
- Up to 90 days after account deletion for security purposes

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** - Access your personal data
- **Rectification** - Correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure** - Delete your data (right to be forgotten)
- **Restriction** - Restrict processing
- **Portability** - Receive your data in structured format
- **Objection** - Object to processing
- **Lodge a Complaint** - File a complaint with the supervisory authority

## 8. How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise the rights mentioned above, contact the data controller through:
- Email: privacy@company.com
- Contact form in the service
- Postal address provided by the data controller

## 9. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, loss, or destruction.

## 10. International Transfers

Some data may be transferred to third countries with appropriate safeguards according to GDPR regulations.

## 11. Changes to This Notice

We reserve the right to update this notice. We will notify you of material changes.

## 12. Contact Information

For any questions regarding your data processing:
- **Data Protection Officer**: dpo@company.com
- **Supervisory Authority**: Your country's data protection authority
