Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: March 2025
Version: 1.0

1. Introduction & Scope

This Refund & Cancellation Policy ("Policy") explains your rights to cancel your eChatbot subscription, request refunds, and our process for handling refund requests. This Policy is part of our Terms of Service and Binding Agreement.

Key Principle: eChatbot values customer satisfaction. We offer refunds only under specific circumstances outlined below. You can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalty, but cancellation does NOT automatically result in a refund.

Disclaimer: No refunds for service periods already consumed (messages sent, customers served, AI responses processed). If you're unhappy, please contact support@echatbot.ai first—we may offer alternatives.

2. Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a refund ONLY in these circumstances:

2.1 Trial Period (First 30 Days)

  • Eligibility: All new accounts receive a 14-day free trial + €19 credit (total €19 value)
  • If you upgrade to a paid plan during trial: You have 30 days from first paid subscription to request a full refund
  • Refund Amount: Full amount paid for the plan (€19, €39, or €140)
  • Condition: Must be requested within 30 days of first charge
  • Non-Refundable During Trial: Any usage fees (messages, orders) are NOT refundable if incurred during your trial period

2.2 Billing Errors

  • Eligibility: If you were charged twice for the same subscription, overcharged, or billed after cancellation
  • Refund Amount: The erroneous charge(s) only. Pro-rata credits for legitimate usage fees
  • Deadline: Must be reported within 30 days of the incorrect charge
  • Process: Contact billing@echatbot.ai with proof of the error (screenshot, transaction ID)

2.3 Service Failures (Outages > 24 Hours)

  • Eligibility: If eChatbot experiences unplanned downtime exceeding 24 consecutive hours during your billing month
  • Refund Amount: Pro-rata credit based on downtime percentage (see Section 4 for formula)
  • Automatic Processing: We monitor uptime 24/7. Credits are applied automatically; no request needed
  • Notification: You'll receive an email with downtime details and credit amount

2.4 Explicit Non-Refundable Items

The following are EXPLICITLY NON-REFUNDABLE regardless of circumstance:

  • Setup fees or onboarding services
  • Custom development or integration work
  • Premium support add-ons (if purchased separately)
  • Already-consumed services (messages sent, orders processed, AI responses generated)
  • Overages for usage-based charges (messages exceeding plan limits)
  • Annual subscriptions after 1st month
  • Chargebacks (see Section 7)

3. Refund Request Process

3.1 Step-by-Step Process

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us: Email refunds@echatbot.ai with the subject "Refund Request - [Your Name]"
  2. Provide Details:
    • Your workspace name and account email
    • Date of the charge
    • Reason for refund (trial period, billing error, or service failure)
    • Screenshot of the transaction (if applicable)
  3. Wait for Review: We review all requests within 7 business days
  4. Approval/Denial: You'll receive an email with the decision and explanation
  5. Payment Processing: Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method (PayPal) within 5-10 business days
  6. Confirmation: You'll receive a PayPal receipt confirming the refund

3.2 Response Times

  • Initial Review: 7 business days (non-urgent requests) or 2 business days (service outage refunds)
  • Payment Processing: 5-10 business days from approval (depends on PayPal processing time)
  • Total Time: Expect 12-17 days from request to money back in your account

4. Pro-Rata Credits & Calculation

For partial month refunds (service failures, cancellation mid-cycle), we calculate pro-rata credits:

4.1 Pro-Rata Formula

Refundable Amount = (Remaining Days ÷ Days in Billing Period) × Monthly Fee

Example:
- You paid €39/month on Jan 1
- Service failed on Jan 10-15 (failed for 5 days)
- Remaining days = 31 - 5 = 26
- Pro-rata credit = (26 ÷ 31) × €39 = €32.65
- You receive: €32.65 credit

4.2 Credit Expiration

  • Pro-rata credits are valid for 90 days from issuance
  • You can use credits for future subscriptions or usage fees
  • Credits cannot be withdrawn as cash
  • Credits do NOT roll over after 90 days; they expire
  • If you cancel during the credit period, unused credits are forfeited (non-refundable)

4.3 Usage Fee Credits

  • For overages: If you were overcharged for messages/orders due to rate limit error, we issue a usage credit
  • Application: Credits are applied to your next invoice automatically
  • Same 90-day expiration applies

5. Cancellation Policy

5.1 How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time, no questions asked:

  1. Log in to your eChatbot dashboard
  2. Go to Settings → Subscription → Cancel Plan
  3. Confirm cancellation
  4. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately

Alternatively, email billing@echatbot.ai with "Cancel subscription - [workspace name]"

5.2 Cancellation Effective Date

  • Immediate Access Loss: Your workspace becomes read-only (no more messages sent or orders processed)
  • Billing Effective Date: Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle
  • No Partial Refund: You are NOT charged for remaining days, but you also receive NO refund for pre-paid periods

5.3 Data Retention After Cancellation

  • Retention Period: 90 days after cancellation
  • Access During Retention: Your data is stored but NOT accessible via the eChatbot platform
  • Export Option: You can request a data export (JSON format) within 30 days of cancellation
  • Permanent Deletion: After 90 days, all your data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered
  • Exception: Tax records (orders, invoices) are retained for 10 years per Italian law

5.4 Reactivation

  • You can reactivate your account within 90 days of cancellation (same workspace)
  • You'll be charged the plan price when you reactivate
  • After 90 days, your workspace is permanently deleted

6. Chargeback & Dispute Policy

6.1 What is a Chargeback?

A chargeback occurs when you dispute a charge with your bank/PayPal instead of contacting eChatbot support directly. Chargebacks harm both us and the entire payment ecosystem.

6.2 eChatbot's Chargeback Defense Process

When a chargeback is filed against us, we:

  1. Investigate: Gather evidence (account creation date, login history, IP address, messages sent, work performed)
  2. Defend: Submit documentation to PayPal proving the service was delivered
  3. Outcome: ~50% of chargebacks are overturned in our favor (depends on evidence relevance)
  4. If Lost: You are charged a €15-25 fee per chargeback (PayPal's processing fee, which we recover from you)
  5. Pattern Abuse: Multiple chargebacks result in account permanent suspension (blacklist)

6.3 Before Filing a Chargeback

PLEASE contact us first:

  • Email support@echatbot.ai with your concern
  • We respond within 2 business days
  • We'll investigate and offer a solution (refund, credit, or repair)
  • Filing a chargeback WITHOUT contacting support first may result in account suspension

6.4 Chargeback Fee & Account Suspension

  • €15-25 fee: Charged to your account for each chargeback filed (PayPal processing cost)
  • 1st Chargeback: Fee applies + decision warning
  • 2nd Chargeback: Fee applies + account suspension for 30 days (read-only)
  • 3rd+ Chargeback: Immediate permanent suspension (deletion of workspace)
  • Pattern Abuse: If you file 3+ chargebacks in 12 months, you are permanently blacklisted

Exception: If we lost your chargeback defense and agreed to a refund, the chargeback fee is waived.

7. Data Deletion Upon Refund (GDPR Compliance)

When you request a refund, here's what happens to your data:

  • Immediate Action: Your workspace is marked for deletion (becomes read-only)
  • 90-Day Retention: Your data is stored in our secure archive but NOT used for any operations
  • Permanent Deletion: After 90 days, all your data is deleted irreversibly (cryptographic key destruction)
  • Exceptions (NOT deleted):
    • Order records (10 years for tax compliance)
    • Invoices and payment records (10 years for tax compliance)
    • Legal hold data (if required by law enforcement)
  • GDPR Compliance: This aligns with GDPR Article 17 (Right to Erasure) and Article 5 (Data Minimization)

8. Appeals & Dispute Resolution

8.1 Appealing a Denied Refund Request

If your refund request was denied, you have 30 days to appeal:

  1. Email refunds@echatbot.ai with subject "Appeal: Refund Request - [Your Name]"
  2. Explain why you believe the denial was incorrect
  3. Provide any new evidence (e.g., proof of service failure, billing screenshots)
  4. We'll review the appeal with fresh eyes within 7 business days
  5. Final decision is binding

8.2 Claims >€500: Binding Arbitration

For refund disputes exceeding €500:

  • Both parties agree to pursue arbitration instead of litigation
  • Arbitration is faster and cheaper than court
  • An independent arbitrator reviews evidence and makes a binding decision
  • Each party bears their own costs; arbitrator fees split 50/50
  • Arbitration award is enforceable like a court judgment

8.3 Claims <€500: Regular Court Process

  • You may pursue Italian courts (Tribunale di Milano, exclusive jurisdiction)
  • Italian law applies
  • See our Terms of Service for full dispute resolution details
