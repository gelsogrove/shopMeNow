Refund & Cancellation Policy

eChatbot (echatbot.ai)

Version 2.1 — Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction & Scope

This Refund & Cancellation Policy ("Policy") governs your rights to cancel your eChatbot subscription and request refunds. eChatbot is an AI-powered conversational commerce platform (the "Service") operated from Spain. The subscriber is a business operator ("Workspace Operator" or "Merchant") who uses the Service to run WhatsApp-based AI chatbots for their shop. This Policy forms part of our Terms of Service.

Nature of the Service: eChatbot is not pure software. The subscription fee covers a bundled managed service comprising AI processing, WhatsApp API integration, platform access, and automated conversation and order management. References to a "subscription" should be understood as access to this managed Service.

Core principle: eChatbot subscriptions are billed monthly via PayPal. You may cancel at any time. Cancellation stops future billing but does not automatically trigger a refund for the current billing period. Refunds are granted under the specific circumstances listed in Section 4.

If you have any issue before requesting a refund, contact us first at echatbotai@gmail.com — we will do our best to find a solution.

2. Governing Law & Consumer Rights

This Policy is governed by Spanish law, in particular the Ley General para la Defensa de los Consumidores y Usuarios (Real Decreto Legislativo 1/2007 — "LGDCU"), and by EU Consumer Rights Directive 2011/83/EU where applicable. Nothing in this Policy limits any mandatory statutory consumer right you may have under those laws.

3. EU Right of Withdrawal — 14-Day Cooling-Off Period

If you are subscribing as a natural person acting outside a purely commercial or professional capacity, you have the right to withdraw from your subscription contract within 14 calendar days of the date the contract was concluded, without giving any reason, in accordance with EU Directive 2011/83/EU and the Spanish LGDCU (Real Decreto Legislativo 1/2007).

Important exception — immediate service commencement: if you expressly request that the Service begin before the 14-day withdrawal period expires and acknowledge that your right of withdrawal will be lost once the Service is fully performed, the right of withdrawal is forfeited proportionally to the extent the Service has already been used. In practice: if you activate your workspace and begin using the chatbot, your right to a full refund under withdrawal law is reduced proportionally to usage already consumed.

To exercise your right of withdrawal, notify us in writing at echatbotai@gmail.com before the 14-day period expires.

4. Refund Eligibility

4.1 30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee (New Subscribers)

All new Workspace Operators are entitled to a full refund if requested within 30 days of their first paid charge, provided:

  • The request is submitted within 30 calendar days of the first subscription charge.
  • The account has not been terminated for breach of Terms of Service (e.g. fraud, abuse, or IP violations — see Section 6).

Refund amount: the full subscription fee paid for the first billing period. Usage-based overages incurred during the 30-day window are non-refundable.

4.2 Billing Errors

You are entitled to a full refund of any erroneous charge, including:

  • Double-billing for the same period.
  • Being charged after cancelling your subscription.
  • Being charged an amount different from the plan price shown at checkout.

Billing error refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of the incorrect charge, with supporting evidence (transaction ID, screenshot). Contact echatbotai@gmail.com.

4.3 Service Outages Exceeding 24 Consecutive Hours

If eChatbot suffers an unplanned platform outage exceeding 24 consecutive hours in any billing month, you are entitled to a pro-rata credit calculated as:

Credit = (Monthly fee ÷ days in billing month) × number of outage days

Credits are applied automatically to your next invoice — no request needed. You will be notified by email with the outage details and credit amount. Planned maintenance windows announced at least 24 hours in advance do not qualify as outages.

4.4 Account Termination by eChatbot Without Cause

If eChatbot terminates your account without cause (i.e. not due to a breach of our Terms of Service on your part), you are entitled to a pro-rata refund for the unused portion of your current billing period.

5. AI Service Limitations — Impact on Refunds

eChatbot provides AI-generated conversational responses "as-is", with commercially reasonable accuracy. Accuracy is not guaranteed. The following rules apply specifically to refunds:

  • No refund for AI content errors. Refunds are not granted on the basis that the AI chatbot delivered incorrect responses to a Merchant's customers — including inaccurate product information, pricing errors, or other content generated by the AI.
  • Merchant review obligation. The Workspace Operator is responsible for reviewing and validating AI-generated content before relying on it commercially. The AI is an assistive tool, not a substitute for human oversight.
  • Customer disputes are the Merchant's responsibility. If an AI response caused a complaint from one of the Merchant's own customers, the Merchant must resolve that matter directly with their customer. eChatbot is not a party to that relationship.
  • Limited liability. In all cases, eChatbot's total liability arising from AI-generated content is limited to the subscription fee paid by the Merchant for the affected billing period.

6. Intellectual Property & Anti-Piracy — Refund Forfeiture

Refunds are void and forfeited where the account was used to scrape, copy, reverse-engineer, or otherwise misappropriate the platform, its software, or its data. Accounts terminated for violations of our intellectual property rights or Terms of Service receive no refund of any kind, including under the 30-day satisfaction guarantee.

7. Non-Refundable Items

The following are explicitly not refundable under any circumstance:

  • Services already consumed: AI responses generated, WhatsApp messages sent, orders processed, and chatbot conversations served.
  • Subscription fees after the 30-day satisfaction period has elapsed (except billing errors and outage credits).
  • Annual subscriptions after the first 30 days of the subscription term.
  • Custom development, integration, or onboarding services.
  • Premium support add-ons purchased separately.
  • Losses attributable to AI content errors (see Section 5).
  • Accounts terminated for IP or Terms of Service violations (see Section 6).
  • Fees forfeited as a result of a chargeback filed with your bank or PayPal (see Section 9).

8. Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your workspace settings or by emailing echatbotai@gmail.com. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. Your workspace remains accessible until that date. After the subscription ends:

  • No further charges are made.
  • Your data is retained for 30 days then permanently deleted per our Privacy Policy.
  • You may export your data before deletion by contacting us.

9. How to Request a Refund

  1. Email echatbotai@gmail.com with subject: Refund Request — [Your Workspace Name].
  2. Include: account email, date of charge, reason for request, supporting evidence (transaction ID or screenshot).
  3. We acknowledge within 2 business days and resolve within 7 business days.
  4. Approved refunds are returned via PayPal to the original payment method. PayPal processing typically takes 3–5 business days.

10. Chargebacks

Filing a chargeback with your bank or PayPal before contacting us is considered a breach of this Policy. We reserve the right to immediately suspend your workspace upon notification of a chargeback. We strongly encourage you to contact us first — we resolve legitimate disputes quickly and without friction.

11. Plan Changes

Upgrades take effect at the next billing date at the new rate. Downgrades take effect at the next billing date at the lower rate. No mid-cycle pro-rata adjustments apply unless agreed in writing.
