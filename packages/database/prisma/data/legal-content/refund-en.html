Refund & Cancellation Policy

1. Refund Policy

eChatbot offers partial or full refunds in case of:

  • Serious service malfunctions
  • Billing errors
  • Cancellation within 14 days (right of withdrawal)

2. Right of Withdrawal (14 days)

You have the right to withdraw within 14 days of purchase without providing reasons. The refund will be:

  • Full if you have not used the service
  • Proportional to actual usage

3. Refunds for Malfunctions

If the service is unavailable for more than 24 consecutive hours, you are entitled to:

  • Proportional refund for downtime
  • Credit for the next month

4. Non-Refundable Credits

The following credits are NOT refundable:

  • Prepaid messages already sent
  • Promotions or bonuses received

5. Refund Procedure

To request a refund:

  1. Send email to billing@echatbot.ai
  2. Include: order number, reason, bank details
  3. You will receive a response within 3 business days
  4. Refund will be processed within 14 days

6. Subscription Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

  • Access backoffice → Settings → Cancel Subscription
  • Service will remain active until the end of the paid period
  • No penalties for early cancellation

7. Contact

For billing questions: billing@echatbot.ai
