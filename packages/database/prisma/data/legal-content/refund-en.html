Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 12 March 2026

1. Scope

This policy applies to subscriptions and usage-based fees purchased directly from echatbot.ai. If you bought through a reseller/marketplace, their terms apply.

2. Free Trials

Trials end automatically at the stated date/usage cap. No charges occur unless you add a payment method and upgrade.

3. Cancellations

  • You can cancel any time from the billing area. Access continues until the end of the current paid term.
  • Cancellations take effect immediately for future renewals; no early termination fees.

4. Refund Eligibility

  • Billing errors or duplicate charges: 100% refund of the erroneous amount.
  • Service unavailability exceeding 24 consecutive hours in a billing period, caused by us and not by third-party networks: pro-rata credit or refund for the affected period.
  • Consumer withdrawal (EU/UK) within 14 days of first purchase: full refund if no meaningful use occurred; otherwise pro-rata for unused days/credits.
  • Non-refundable: one-time onboarding/setup fees already delivered, overage already consumed (messages sent), promotional/bonus credits, bank/FX fees.

5. How to Request

  1. Email billing@echatbot.ai from your account email.
  2. Include invoice ID, workspace ID, and reason.
  3. We acknowledge within 3 business days and resolve within 10 business days. Approved refunds are returned via the original payment method.

6. Chargebacks

Please contact us before initiating a chargeback so we can resolve the issue quickly. Unfounded chargebacks may lead to account suspension.

7. Amendments

We may update this policy; changes apply to future purchases and renewals. Material changes will be communicated in advance when required by law.
