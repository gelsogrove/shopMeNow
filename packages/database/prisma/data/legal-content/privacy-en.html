Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 March 2026

eChatbot (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the echatbot.ai platform, WhatsApp integrations, chat widget, backoffice and related APIs (collectively, the “Service”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and share personal data when you visit or use the Service.

Who this applies to: (1) Platform Users — businesses and developers who create an eChatbot account to run AI-powered channels; (2) End Customers — customers of those businesses who interact via WhatsApp or the chat widget. This policy covers both categories.

1. Data Controller & Contact

Data Controller: eChatbot S.L., Carrer de Balmes 123, 08008 Barcelona, Spain.
Email: privacy@echatbot.ai.
All privacy requests are handled by our compliance team. We will respond within 30 days.

2. Categories of Data We Process

Platform Users

  • Account data: name, email, password hash, workspace name, company details, billing identifiers.
  • Usage & technical data: IP address, browser/device headers, server logs, error traces, event telemetry, session identifiers, cookies.
  • Payment data: processed by Stripe or PayPal. We store only the last 4 digits, card brand, expiry and provider tokens to issue receipts.
  • Support data: tickets, recordings or screenshots you send to our support team.

End Customers

  • Contact data: WhatsApp phone number and display name provided by Meta.
  • Conversation data: messages exchanged with the AI assistant, order history, cart items.
  • Files: images or documents optionally shared during chat.

3. Purpose & Legal Basis

  • Provide the Service (account setup, routing conversations, AI responses, sending notifications) — Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.
  • Billing & fraud prevention — Art. 6(1)(c) and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.
  • Product analytics & reliability (aggregated, pseudonymised where possible) — Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.
  • Marketing communications (email/WhatsApp) only with opt-in consent — Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR; you can opt out anytime.
  • Legal compliance (security logging, retention for tax rules) — Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR.

4. AI and LLM Processing

We use third-party LLM providers (primarily via OpenRouter, which routes to providers such as OpenAI, Anthropic and others) to generate AI responses. We apply the following safeguards:

  • Payment data, passwords and special-category data are never included in LLM prompts.
  • Inputs are processed transiently to generate a response and are not used to train public models.
  • Workspace-specific content (product catalogues, FAQs) injected into prompts is treated as confidential.

AI processing is based on Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest) and Art. 6(1)(b) for Platform Users.

5. Data Sharing & Sub-Processors

  • Cloud infrastructure: Heroku (Salesforce) for application hosting; AWS and OCI datacentres in the EU where available.
  • LLM providers: OpenRouter, OpenAI and other AI providers under EU Standard Contractual Clauses.
  • Messaging: Meta (WhatsApp Business Platform) for message delivery; WAAPI and Twilio as messaging intermediaries.
  • Payments: Stripe and PayPal as independent controllers for card transaction data.
  • Media storage: Cloudinary for image hosting and optimisation.
  • Monitoring & error tracking: Sentry and similar tools with IP masking enabled where available.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share data for advertising purposes.

6. International Transfers

When data leaves the EEA/UK, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and, if needed, additional safeguards (encryption in transit and at rest, access control).

7. Retention

  • Account data: retained while the account is active; deleted within 90 days of closure unless a legal obligation requires longer retention.
  • Conversation transcripts: workspace admins can delete them at any time via the dashboard; otherwise retained for up to 24 months or deleted on request.
  • Billing records: retained for up to 10 years to comply with tax and accounting law.
  • Server logs: typically retained for 30–90 days, then purged.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to: access, rectification, erasure ("right to be forgotten"), restriction, data portability and objection to processing. For consent-based processing, you may withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior lawful processing.

To exercise any right, email privacy@echatbot.ai. We respond within 30 days. You may also lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority (e.g., AEPD in Spain, ICO in the UK, or the Garante in Italy).

California residents (CCPA/CPRA): you have the right to know, delete, correct and opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information. Contact privacy@echatbot.ai.

Brazil (LGPD): you have equivalent rights under Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados. Contact privacy@echatbot.ai to exercise them.

9. Security

We implement technical and organisational measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) for all API and web traffic.
  • Encryption at rest for databases and sensitive file storage.
  • Role-based access control with least-privilege principles.
  • Audit logging for all administrative operations.
  • Regular security reviews and vulnerability assessments.

No method is 100% secure. Please use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever available.

10. Cookies

We use the following categories of cookies:

  • Essential: required for authentication and session management (session tokens, CSRF protection). These cannot be disabled.
  • Functional: remember your preferences such as language selection and workspace.
  • Analytics: aggregate, anonymised usage data to improve the platform. Opt-in where required by law.

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings. Disabling non-essential cookies will not affect core Service functionality.

11. Child Data

The Service is not intended for children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect their personal data. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact privacy@echatbot.ai and we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you by email or via an in-app notice at least 7 days before they take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the Service after a change takes effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
