Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 March 2026

eChatbot (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the echatbot.ai platform, WhatsApp integrations, chat widget, backoffice and related APIs (collectively, the “Service”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and share personal data when you visit or use the Service.

1. Data Controller & Contact

Data Controller: eChatbot S.L., Carrer de Balmes 123, 08008 Barcelona, Spain. Email: privacy@echatbot.ai.

2. Categories of Data We Process

  • Account data: name, email, password hash, workspace details, billing identifiers.
  • Usage & device data: IP, headers, logs, event telemetry, cookies or similar identifiers.
  • Conversation data: messages exchanged through WhatsApp, the website widget or APIs; optional files you upload.
  • Payment data: processed by our PSPs (Stripe/PayPal). We store only tokens, the last 4 digits and expiry to issue receipts.
  • Support data: tickets, recordings or screenshots you provide.

3. Purpose & Legal Basis

  • Provide the Service (account setup, routing conversations, AI responses, sending notifications) — Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.
  • Billing & fraud prevention — Art. 6(1)(c) and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.
  • Product analytics & reliability (aggregated, pseudonymised where possible) — Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.
  • Marketing communications (email/WhatsApp) only with opt-in consent — Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR; you can opt out anytime.
  • Legal compliance (security logging, retention for tax rules) — Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR.

4. AI Processing

We use LLM providers (e.g., OpenAI) to generate answers. We avoid sending payment details or other special categories. Inputs may be temporarily processed to deliver the response but are not used to train public models.

5. Data Sharing

  • Infrastructure: AWS/OCI datacentres in the EU where possible.
  • LLM providers: OpenAI (US/EU) under EU Standard Contractual Clauses.
  • Messaging: Meta (WhatsApp), Twilio/WAAPI for message delivery.
  • Payments: Stripe and PayPal as independent controllers for card data.
  • Analytics/Monitoring: tools such as Sentry or PostHog with IP masking where available.

6. International Transfers

When data leaves the EEA/UK, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and, if needed, additional safeguards (encryption in transit and at rest, access control).

7. Retention

  • Account data: kept while the account is active; deleted within 90 days after closure unless legal retention applies.
  • Conversation transcripts: workspace admins can delete them; otherwise removed after 24 months or on request.
  • Billing records: retained for up to 10 years to comply with tax law.

8. Your Rights

You can request access, rectification, deletion, restriction, portability or objection at any time. For consent-based processing, you may withdraw consent without affecting prior processing. To exercise rights, email privacy@echatbot.ai. You can also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. Security

We use encryption in transit (TLS), encryption at rest for databases, role-based access, audit logging and least-privilege keys. No method is 100% secure; please use strong passwords and 2FA where available.

10. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management, and optional analytics cookies (opt-in where required). See our Cookie Notice for details.

11. Child Data

The Service is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect their data.

12. Changes

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be notified via email or in-app notice. Continued use means you accept the updated Policy.
