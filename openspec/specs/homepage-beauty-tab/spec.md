# homepage-beauty-tab Specification

## Purpose

Defines the third showcase tab on the homepage HomeShowcase component, presenting the Demobeauty beauty-center franchise flow alongside the existing laundry and real-estate tabs.

## Requirements

### Requirement: Third showcase tab on homepage
The HomeShowcase component SHALL display a third tab for Demobeauty alongside the existing laundry and real-estate tabs.

#### Scenario: Beauty tab visible on homepage
- **WHEN** a visitor views the homepage showcase
- **THEN** three tabs are visible: laundry, real-estate, and beauty center

#### Scenario: Beauty tab shows relevant conversation
- **WHEN** the visitor selects the beauty tab
- **THEN** an animated WhatsApp conversation demonstrating the beauty-center franchise flow plays, highlighting features: sede selection, service booking, cart, push notification, and escalation
